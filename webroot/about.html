About Huminic Motors

A family-owned Huminic dealership serving Central Texas since 2004.

We opened our doors in 2004 with a simple idea: buying and servicing a car should be honest, easy, and even enjoyable.

Two decades later, Huminic Motors is still family-owned and still driven by that same principle. We've grown into one of Central Texas's most trusted Huminic dealerships, but we've never lost the neighborhood feel. Our sales team works on your timeline — no pressure, no games — and our service center treats your vehicle like it's their own.

From your first test drive to every oil change after, we're here to make ownership simple. Come say hello at our Innovation Parkway showroom, or reach out anytime and our AI assistant will point you in the right direction.
